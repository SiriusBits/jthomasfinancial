Core rules:
- Do not distort, skew, or stretch logos.
- Maintain clear space around the logo.
- Do not apply drop shadows, bevels, or decorative effects.
- Do not alter logo colors outside approved palette.
- Navy should dominate; gold is an accent color.

Primary brand colors:
- Navy: #0B2238
- Gold: #C7A24D
- Slate / Charcoal: #2A3B4C
- Warm Off-White: #EDEBE7

Typography:

- Body: Inter
